About

I am an Associate Physical AI Engineer at Rose City Robotics, focusing on real-world robot learning and robust estimation. My research centers on Bayesian perception, simulation-based inference, and continuous normalizing flows to build robots that can reason under uncertainty.

Previously, I was a 2025 NSF RTG Fellow working on Bayesian state estimation for automated EV battery disassembly. I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science from Portland State University in 2025, where my work on multi-agent LLM simulations was published in the Northeast Journal of Complex Systems.

Looking forward, I plan to start my PhD in Fall 2027 to develop the next generation of uncertainty-aware perception frameworks for physical robots.

Research

Uncertainty-aware perception for robotic EV battery disassembly

NSF RTG research investigating bimodal pose distributions under heavy occlusion. Implemented Sequential Neural Posterior Estimation (SNPE) inside an Isaac Sim environment to construct calibrated belief states for robotic tool alignment.

GitHub · Video Demo

Unitree G1 grocery-loading autonomy

A full perception–estimation–policy–control loop on the Unitree G1: locate a grocery bag, navigate to it, grasp it, load it into a car, and return — all driven by calibrated state estimates that update with live feedback during each action.

Multi-agent LLM simulation of Secret Hitler

Developed a simulation framework evaluating social deduction and deceptive behavior in large language models. Implemented private Chain-of-Thought reasoning alongside public communication channels to study emergent coordination.

GitHub · Paper (NEJCS)

Image-conditioned posterior diffusion

A conditional diffusion model trained to sample p(center | frame) over the bag's location from a single camera image. As the bag is occluded or leaves the view, its belief widens from a tight mode to a ring of hypotheses — matching the true Monte-Carlo posterior to within about a pixel of spread, where a least-squares estimate collapses to one impossible point. Calibration verified with simulation-based calibration.

GitHub

Flow matching from scratch

Implementation of Continuous Normalizing Flows (CNFs) using flow matching objectives. Evaluated alignment with MolmoBot action-head trajectory predictions for robotic trajectory smoothing.
